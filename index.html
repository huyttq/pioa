Perth Institute of Australia

Perth Institute of Australia (PIA) is a registered CRICOS Provider (Commonwealth Register of International Courses for Overseas Students Number Code 04049F) and welcomes International students to study in Perth on student visas.

As Western Australia’s private Registered Training Organisation (RTO Code 45898), Perth Institute of Australia (PIA) offers specialist training programs in Kitchen Management (formerly known as Commercial Cookery), Business, Leadership and Management.

Our courses include:

  • SIT40521 Cert IV in Kitchen Management
  • SIT50422 Diploma of Hospitality Management
  • SIT60322Diploma of Hospitality Management
  • BSB40120 Cert IV in Business
  • BSB50120 Diploma of Business
  • BSB60120 Advanced Diploma of Leadership and Management

PIA is a Perth based Training Provider specialising in creating opportunities through practical learning and structures training around a number of core values including passion, integrity, drive, and commitment for your personal and career growth. The course structure involves compulsory work based training or practical placements in approved facility, giving students real hands-on workplace experience while studying.

PIA is located on unit 16, 200 Wellington Street, East Perth WA 6004. The premise is readily accessible by public transport with McIver train station is only 300m away and Free Yellow Cat from Perth city centre (3 stops). Cafes, restaurants, parks are within walking distance. Students are provided with a range of high-quality facilities and training kitchens. Modern training rooms, computer equipment and Wi-Fi are available throughout the campus, Academic and personal counselling services are also provided, free of charge to our students.
